# Canva Pro Feature Research & Analysis

## Core Design Engine
- **Drag-and-Drop Editor**: Intuitive canvas for placing, resizing, and layering elements.
- **Magic Resize**: One-click adaptation of designs to different social media and print dimensions.
- **Layers & Groups**: Advanced management of design elements.
- **Brand Kits**: Centralized storage for logos, colors, and fonts.

## AI & Magic Studio Features
- **Magic Grab**: Extract and move subjects within a photo.
- **Magic Expand**: AI-powered background extension.
- **Magic Edit**: Swap or add elements in photos via text prompts.
- **Magic Eraser**: Remove unwanted objects from images.
- **Background Remover**: Instant removal for both photos and videos.
- **Magic Write**: AI copy generation and brand voice consistency.
- **Canva AI Assistant**: Conversational AI for generating logos, posts, and print designs.
- **Canva Code**: AI-powered creation of interactive experiences (portfolios, trackers) without coding.
- **Beat Sync**: Automatic video synchronization to music rhythm.

## Asset Library
- **Premium Content**: Access to millions of high-quality photos, videos, and audio tracks.
- **Royalty-Free Collections**: Partnerships with Alamy, Artlist, pocstock, and Disney.
- **Templates**: Professional templates for social media, presentations, documents, and flyers.

## Automation & Integration
- **Magic Studio at Scale**: Bulk content creation using data from sheets.
- **App Integrations**: Connections with LinkedIn Ads, Slack, and other workflow tools.
- **Style Match**: Applying styles from one element to another instantly.

## User Request Specifics
- **Manus API Integration**: Every human action must be executable by Manus.
- **VS Code Bridge**: Designing to deploying transition.
- **System Integration**: Full automation of processes after learning.
